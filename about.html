Meet Michael – The Charleston Entertainer Who Brings It All

Michael is the featured performer behind Mount Pleasant Male Strippers, offering a unique and upscale private show experience for Charleston and the surrounding Lowcountry. He’s not part of a large group or national brand — he’s an independent entertainer who focuses on quality, personality, and connection. Whether your event is a small Airbnb party, a downtown Charleston girls’ night, or a beach celebration near Sullivan’s Island, Michael brings a personalized touch that’s confident, classy, and unforgettable.

With years of experience performing across the Southeast, Michael blends choreographed performance with natural crowd chemistry. His shows are designed to keep guests laughing, cheering, and fully engaged — all while keeping the experience professional and respectful. He takes pride in helping groups celebrate life’s biggest moments, whether it’s a bachelorette weekend, birthday bash, or “just because” night of fun.

What sets Michael apart is his genuine Charleston hospitality and commitment to making every show memorable. From Mount Pleasant’s waterfront homes to downtown lofts, he tailors his performance to each venue and audience. He’s also active online, so guests can preview his style and personality through his TikTok, Facebook, and The Knot profiles.
